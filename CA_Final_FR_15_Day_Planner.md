# CA Final FR — 15-Day Study Planner

**Subject:** Financial Reporting (Paper 1, CA Final — New/Old Scheme)
**Daily commitment assumed:** 6–7 hours (adjust to your pace)
**Approach:** Conceptual clarity → Problem practice → Revision → Mock tests

---

## Week 1: Core Standards & Foundations

### Day 1 — Framework & Ind AS 1, 2, 7
- Conceptual Framework for Financial Reporting
- Ind AS 1 (Presentation of FS), Ind AS 2 (Inventories), Ind AS 7 (Cash Flow Statement)
- Solve 8–10 practice problems on Cash Flow Statement

### Day 2 — Ind AS 8, 10, 12
- Ind AS 8 (Accounting Policies, Estimates, Errors)
- Ind AS 10 (Events after Reporting Period)
- Ind AS 12 (Income Taxes) — focus on deferred tax computations
- Practice 6–8 sums on deferred tax

### Day 3 — Ind AS 16, 23, 40
- Ind AS 16 (Property, Plant & Equipment) — componentization, revaluation
- Ind AS 23 (Borrowing Costs)
- Ind AS 40 (Investment Property)
- Practice 8–10 problems combining PPE + borrowing cost capitalization

### Day 4 — Ind AS 36, 37, 38
- Ind AS 36 (Impairment of Assets) — CGU, recoverable amount
- Ind AS 37 (Provisions, Contingent Liabilities & Assets)
- Ind AS 38 (Intangible Assets)
- Practice impairment and provisioning case studies

### Day 5 — Ind AS 19, 20, 102
- Ind AS 19 (Employee Benefits) — actuarial valuation basics, defined benefit plans
- Ind AS 20 (Government Grants)
- Ind AS 102 (Share-Based Payment)
- Practice 6–8 numericals (especially Ind AS 19 and 102)

### Day 6 — Ind AS 105, 41, 116
- Ind AS 105 (Non-current Assets Held for Sale & Discontinued Operations)
- Ind AS 41 (Agriculture)
- Ind AS 116 (Leases) — lessee/lessor accounting, ROU asset
- Practice lease accounting problems thoroughly (high weightage)

### Day 7 — Revision Day 1
- Revise Days 1–6 using self-made notes/summary charts
- Re-attempt 2–3 problems from each standard you found difficult
- Take a short standard-wise quiz (recall journal entries & disclosure points)

---

## Week 2: Financial Instruments, Consolidation & Special Topics

### Day 8 — Ind AS 32, 109, 107
- Ind AS 32 (Financial Instruments: Presentation)
- Ind AS 109 (Financial Instruments: Recognition & Measurement) — EIR, ECL model, classification
- Ind AS 107 (Disclosures) — conceptual only
- Practice EIR and ECL-based problems (very scoring but tricky)

### Day 9 — Ind AS 113, 33, 24
- Ind AS 113 (Fair Value Measurement)
- Ind AS 33 (Earnings Per Share)
- Ind AS 24 (Related Party Disclosures)
- Practice EPS numericals (basic & diluted)

### Day 10 — Consolidation Basics: Ind AS 110
- Ind AS 110 (Consolidated Financial Statements)
- Concepts: control, NCI, goodwill/capital reserve computation
- Solve 3–4 full consolidation problems (subsidiary only)

### Day 11 — Consolidation Advanced: Ind AS 28, 111
- Ind AS 28 (Investments in Associates & Joint Ventures) — equity method
- Ind AS 111 (Joint Arrangements)
- Mixed group problems: subsidiary + associate
- Solve 2–3 problems combining step acquisition / disposal

### Day 12 — Business Combinations & Corporate Restructuring
- Ind AS 103 (Business Combinations) — purchase consideration, goodwill, bargain purchase
- Amalgamation/Demerger accounting (Schedule III & relevant standards)
- Practice 4–5 numericals on business combinations

### Day 13 — Integrated Reporting, Valuation & Recent Amendments
- Integrated Reporting framework
- Valuation of goodwill and shares (if applicable in your syllabus version)
- Recent ICAI amendments/announcements for FR (check latest study material/RTP)
- NBFC & Bank financial statements (if applicable in your scheme)

### Day 14 — Full Revision Day
- Revise all 21+ Ind AS — use summary charts/flowcharts
- Redo all formula-based areas: EPS, deferred tax, lease, consolidation, EIR/ECL
- Attempt one full-length question paper (previous attempt or RTP) under timed conditions

### Day 15 — Mock Test & Final Touch-up
- Attempt 1 full mock test (3 hours) — ideally latest RTP/MTP
- Evaluate mistakes, revisit only weak standards
- Quick glance through disclosure requirements and theory-based standards (Ind AS 1, 8, 24, 37)
- Prepare a one-page "last-minute revision" formula/concept sheet

---

## Tips for Effective Use
- **Practice over theory**: FR is largely numerical — prioritize solving problems over passive reading.
- **Maintain a standard-wise formula book**: especially for deferred tax, EPS, leases, EIR/ECL, and consolidation.
- **Use ICAI Study Material + RTPs/MTPs** as your primary problem source; this planner assumes you're working through them alongside.
- **High-weightage areas** typically include: Consolidation, Financial Instruments, Ind AS 116 (Leases), Ind AS 103 (Business Combinations), and Ind AS 19 — allocate extra revision time if your attempt is approaching.
- Adjust days for Ind AS 101 (First-time Adoption) or NBFC/Ind AS for banks if these are part of your specific syllabus version.
